About Us

Hi there!
We are Sarah Cross and Shreya Chaudhary, two high schoolers looking to make a difference in the world. We met when were in middle school, and we were the only two females who signed up for the school's new robotics elective. We were instantly friends and bonded over our love for technology and making the society a better place to help those we love. Ever since middle school, we have been working together, striving to better our world one line of code at a time.

Our Story

The story goes back to about two years ago, when one of our siblings was diagnosed with Crohn's disease. What made the situation incredibly difficult was the level of uncertainity. What foods would trigger a gastrointestinal reaction? Which ones wouldn't? There are a lot of suggested diets out there which follow what generally causes these problems; however, a lot of this comes down to you. There is no one answer, no one cure. Everyone is different and the bacteria in our colon is different. So we created InflaTracker to help.

Quick Disclaimer

We created this application to help with inflammation. It uses a machine learning algorithm to classify foods that tend to hurt your stomach versus ones that don't. However, there is a level of experimentation in this. This is meant for people who are willing to put themselves out there, try new things, and help the algorithm learn. We highly recommend to consult your doctor first before using our application.
